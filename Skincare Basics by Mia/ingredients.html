Understanding Common Skincare Ingredients

Educational guide by Mia

Skincare products will often list dozens of complicated ingredients, which can make choosing the correct products feel super overwhelming. Understanding a few common skincare ingredients can help consumers make smart and informed decisions to avoid uneccesary skin irritation.

Why ingredients matter

Skincare ingredients determine how a product will function, who the product works best for, and how it might interact with your skin or with other products. Using certain ingredients incorrectly or too frequently is a common cause of skin irritation or breakouts.

Common skincare ingredients

Ingredient Best for Purpose
Niacinamide Redness, oil control Strengthens the skin barrier and balances oil production
Hyaluronic Acid Dehydration Attracts moisture to keep skin hydrated
Salicylic Acid Acne-prone skin Clears pores and reduces breakouts
Retinol Texture, fine lines Overtime it encourages skin cell turnover

Beginner tips for using ingredients

  • Introduce your skin to one new ingredient at a time

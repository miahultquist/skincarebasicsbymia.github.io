Skincare 101

Why the basics of skincare matter

IST250 Final Project by Mia

I've found that a lot of people feel overwhelmed by skincare, especially in today's society, because of trends, random social media advice, and overly complex routines. I believe that understanding the basics of skincare helps to prevent irritated skin and wasted money.

Beginner skincare goals

  • Protect the skin barrier
  • Keep skin hydrated
  • Use sunscreen daily

These 3 goals will help make skincare feel manageable and consistent, instead of stressful or irritating.

Common skincare mistakes

Burning skin does not mean your skincare is working.

Over-exfoliating, using too many actives at one time, and not applying moisturizer are some of the most common mistakes made by skincare beginners.

What to avoid

  1. Introducing your skin to multiple new products at once
  2. Using harsh exfoliating scrubs daily
  3. Skipping sunscreen application
